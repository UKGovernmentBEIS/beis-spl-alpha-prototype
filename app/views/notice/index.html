Apply for shared parental leave and pay

You can use this service to complete the notices and declarations required to take shared parental leave and pay.

These forms can be completed online, but you’ll need to print, sign and give them to your employer 8 weeks before your first block of shared parental leave starts. Your employer may have their own forms they’d prefer you to use.

Both parents need to give certain notices and declarations for either parent to take shared parental leave and pay.

Before you start, you'll need to know:

  • If you can get shared parental leave and pay
  • How you intend to take shared parental leave and pay
