Check if you can get leave or pay when you have a child

As parents, you can get:

  • Maternity Leave
  • Maternity Pay
  • Paternity Leave
  • Paternity Pay
  • Shared Parental Leave
  • Shared Parental Pay

Maternity leave

The mother can take up to 52 weeks of maternity leave. Unused maternity leave can be converted into shared parental leave.

Shared parental leave

The mother and her partner can take shared parental leave (SPL). It lasts for up to 52 weeks, minus any weeks of maternity leave taken by the mother.

The parents can take this leave in multiple blocks, going back to work between each block. They can take leave at the same time, or one after the other.

Maternity leave must be taken as one continuous block. When you return to work, maternity leave ends. Shared parental leave can be taken in multiple blocks, with periods of work in between. The mother and her partner can take shared parental leave at the same time or one after the other.

Use the shared parental leave planning tool to work out how to take your leave.

The mother and her partner can both get shared parental pay. It lasts for up to 39 weeks, minus any weeks of maternity pay or maternity allowance the mother has received.
