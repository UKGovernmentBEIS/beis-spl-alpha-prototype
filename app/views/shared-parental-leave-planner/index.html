Plan leave and pay when you have a child

Shared parental leave is available for parents who are employed and plan to care for their child with their partner.

Shared parental pay is available for up to 37 weeks and is paid at £148.68 (or 90% of your average weekly salary if you earn less than £165.20 per week). This is the same as the last 33 weeks of maternity pay.

Use this tool to plan how to take time off work when you have a child.
