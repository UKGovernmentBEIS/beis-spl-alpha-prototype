Parental leave and pay planner

Shared parental leave is available for parents who are employed and planning on caring for their child with their partner. It is available for up to 50 weeks, minus any maternity leave taken by the mother. Parents can choose how to split the available leave between them. It can be taken in blocks, with periods of work in between, so that childcare can be shared more equally between parents in the first year of their child’s life or placement if adopting.

Statutory Shared Parental Pay is paid at £145.18 (or 90% of your average weekly salary if you earn less than £161.31 per week).
