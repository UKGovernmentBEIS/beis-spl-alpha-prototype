Examples of leave plans: Managing parenting and work

Background

The mother wants to return to work for a project in the middle of her maternity leave. She decides to end her maternity leave and convert her remaining entitlement into shared parent leave. This allows her partner to take 3 months off work while she attends to her project. It also allows her to resume her time at home with their child after her project is over.

They would like to take some leave together. The partner's first month of shared parental leave will overlap with the mother's last month of maternity leave.

Leave plan

  • Mother's leave and pay: 35 weeks maternity leave and statutory maternity pay, 4 weeks shared parental leave and statutory shared parental pay.
  • Partner's leave and pay: 2 weeks paternity leave and statutory paternity pay and 12 weeks shared parental leave.
